Installation instructions: 

1) Make a backup copy of the \GameData\NavyFish\ folder
2) Extract the contents of the zip archive to \GameData\NavyFish\Plugins\PluginData\DockingPortAlignment
3) When prompted to overwrite, confirm overwriting
4) Launch KSP and test the new textures.
